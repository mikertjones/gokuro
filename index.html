Gokuro

How to play

  • Gokuro puzzle is a word grid
  • There are no repeated words
  • The sum of letter values in each word is shown to the right and below
  • A '*' in a square indicates a vowel
  • A '!' after a word total indicates that at least one letter is used more than once in the word - not neccessarily as a double
  • The letter tray below the grid shows how many of each letter appears in the grid and their values

Tips

  • start with the lowest word total
  • test with vowels to see what total remains to be found
